Project Highlights

ScaleSplat is a scalable, SfM-free 3D Gaussian Splatting framework. It addresses the key challenge of scalable 3D reconstruction under practical hardware constraints through a memory–representation co-design. This enables a single, unified model to operate effectively across sparse-view, dense-view, and large-scale scenarios on consumer-grade GPUs.

Scalable SfM-free 3D Gaussian Splatting via Memory‑Representation Co‑Design

Abstract

ScaleSplat is a scalable, SfM-free 3D Gaussian Splatting framework designed to overcome hardware constraints in 3D reconstruction. Through a novel memory–representation co-design, it enables a single model to operate efficiently across sparse-view, dense-view, and large-scale scenarios on consumer-grade GPUs. This work makes high-fidelity, scalable 3D reconstruction practical for real-world multimedia applications.
